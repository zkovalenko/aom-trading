<h2>1. Market Structure Tool (MST) – The Foundation of Wave Formation</h2>
<p>
  The Market Structure Tool (MST) provides the base for Wave Formation.
  It defines the architecture of price movement and connects short-term market structures ("Micro Moves")
  with long-term directional cycles ("Macro Waves").
  By connecting Waves' Highs and Lows, MST identifies the High-Level Market Structure and outlines the dominant trend.
</p>

<h3>Key Functions:</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Maps structural highs and lows to reveal trend continuity or transitions.</li>
  <li>Distinguishes between micro corrections and true structural breaks, helping traders stay longer in the trend.</li>
</ul>

<blockquote style="background: #f8f9fa; border-left: 4px solid #007bff; padding: 1rem 1.5rem; margin: 1.5rem 0; font-style: italic; color: #2c3e50;">
  <strong>AOM Insight:</strong> "Micro Move defines structure; Macro Move defines wave.
  MST connects them — turning short-term rhythm into long-term clarity."
</blockquote>

<h2>3. Wave Analyzer, State Machine, and Trading Setups</h2>
<p>The Wave Analyzer and State Machine interpret market rhythm and behavioral phase:</p>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>Wave Analyzer</strong> identifies and labels active wave states — Expansion, Formation, or Reversal.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>State Machine</strong> tracks market conditions in real time and signals when transitions occur.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Trading Setups</strong> (New Zone Formation, New Wave Expansion, New Wave Reversal) are triggered
    when wave and structure conditions align.
  </li>
</ul>
<p>
  Together, they translate market data into defined opportunity states, creating rule-based setups.
</p>

<h2>4. Built-in NT Predefined ATM – Automated Trade Management System</h2>
<p>The ATM module governs post-entry execution:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Places and adjusts stop-loss and targets automatically.</li>
  <li>Resolves Partial fills.</li>
  <li>Maintains risk consistency across all trades.</li>
  <li>Enforces discipline through automation.</li>
</ul>
<p>
  <strong>Purpose:</strong> Remove emotional influence by allowing system logic to manage position life-cycle.
</p>

<h2>5. Semi-Automated Order Entry Panel (OEP)</h2>
<p>The OEP acts as the bridge between discretion and automation:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Enables selection of active Supply or Demand Zones.</li>
  <li>Confirms trade direction (Long/Short).</li>
  <li>Links to the ATM profile for automatic management.</li>
  <li>Allows the trader to adjust entry interactively while adhering to rule logic.</li>
</ul>
<p>
  <strong>Purpose:</strong> Provide controlled flexibility — the trader defines the context; the system enforces discipline.
</p>
